Braised Beef

Description

  • Beef
  • Onion and garlic
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Mushrooms
  • Carrots
  • Leaves

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients and preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Season beef.
  3. Heat oil in a large skillet.
  4. Stir in wine and scrape to release any more bits from the pan.
